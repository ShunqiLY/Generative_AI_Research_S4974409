Research Proposal - Draft v1
Title: Using Generative AI Tools - Boon or Bane
Date: 2026-04-29

1. Research Problem
Generative AI tools such as ChatGPT are rapidly being adopted in academic and professional
settings. Despite their potential benefits, concerns persist around accuracy, ethics, and
academic integrity. This study investigates how users perceive and navigate these tensions.

2. Research Questions
RQ1: What are the primary benefits and challenges experienced by users of GenAI tools?
RQ2: How do users' roles (student vs. professional) shape their perceptions of GenAI?
RQ3: What strategies do users employ to manage integrity and trust concerns?

3. Methodology
Mixed-methods design:
  - Quantitative: Online survey (target n=100) with Likert-scale items.
  - Qualitative: Semi-structured interviews (target n=12) analysed thematically.

4. Expected Outcomes
Findings will inform institutional policy on GenAI use and contribute to the emerging
literature on responsible AI adoption in education and professional practice.
