Survey Analysis Report
Study: Using Generative AI Tools - Boon or Bane
Date: 2026-04-29
Status: Draft

Summary of Findings
-------------------
- Majority of respondents use GenAI tools daily or weekly.
- Most common use case: writing assistance and code generation.
- Average productivity score: 4.4/5 — respondents generally view GenAI as productive.
- Ethical concerns (Q6) rated highly (avg 3.4/5), indicating awareness of risks.
- Trust in GenAI outputs is moderate (avg 3.0/5).
- Academic integrity concerns are present but vary by role.
