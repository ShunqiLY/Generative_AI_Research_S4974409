Participant Information Sheet
Study: Using Generative AI Tools - Boon or Bane
Institution: The University of Queensland
Date: 2026-04-29

You are invited to participate in a research study examining how people perceive and use
Generative AI tools in academic and professional settings.

What will the study involve?
  - Completing an online survey (approx. 10 minutes), OR
  - Participating in a recorded interview (approx. 30-40 minutes)

Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without consequence.

All data collected will be de-identified and stored securely. Only the research team will
have access to raw data. Published findings will not identify individual participants.

If you have questions about this study, please contact the research team at:
