Qualitative Insights Report
Study: Using Generative AI Tools - Boon or Bane
Date: 2026-04-29
Status: Draft

Key Themes Identified (Thematic Analysis)
------------------------------------------
Theme 1: Productivity and Efficiency
  - Participants widely reported time savings as the primary benefit.
  - GenAI tools used as "thought partners" to overcome creative blocks.

Theme 2: Trust and Accuracy Concerns
  - Hallucination / confident misinformation was the most cited challenge.
  - All participants described verification strategies.

Theme 3: Academic and Professional Integrity
  - Students showed awareness of policies and self-regulated usage.
  - Industry participants reported fewer formal constraints.

Theme 4: Evolving Adoption Patterns
  - Usage tends to increase over time as familiarity grows.
  - Early sceptics became regular users after positive experiences.

Methodology Notes
-----------------
- Coding framework: Inductive thematic analysis (Braun & Clarke, 2006)
- Inter-rater reliability check pending second coder.
- Data saturation expected at n=12 interviews.
