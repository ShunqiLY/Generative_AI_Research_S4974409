Interview Protocol
Study: Using Generative AI Tools - Boon or Bane
Version: 1.0
Date: 2026-04-29

Introduction (2 min)
- Introduce the research team and study purpose.
- Confirm participant consent and permission to record.
- Remind participant that participation is voluntary and they may withdraw at any time.

Section 1: Background (5 min)
1. Can you briefly describe your role and how long you have been in this role?
2. How did you first encounter Generative AI tools in your work or studies?

Section 2: Usage Patterns (10 min)
3. Which Generative AI tools do you currently use? How often?
4. What tasks do you primarily use them for?
5. Has your use of these tools changed over time? If so, how?

Section 3: Benefits and Challenges (15 min)
6. What benefits have you experienced from using GenAI tools?
7. Have you encountered any challenges or problems? Can you give an example?
8. How do you evaluate the quality or trustworthiness of GenAI outputs?

Section 4: Ethics and Integrity (10 min)
9. Do you have any concerns about academic or professional integrity when using GenAI?
10. How do your institution or organisation's policies address GenAI use?

Closing (3 min)
- Ask if there is anything else the participant would like to add.
- Thank the participant and explain next steps.
